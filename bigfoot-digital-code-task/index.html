About The Dental Clinic

The Dental Clinic has been dedicated to providing exemplary service and personalized dental care in Leeds & the rest of Yorkshire.

Through a commitment to proven therapies and the latest technology, The Dental Clinic has risen to the forefront of modern dentistry. We are regularly featured in magazines and on tv.

The Dental Clinic is proud to offer a luxurious, spa-like environment where you’ll be pampered and listened to. Every appointment revolves around perfecting your beautiful smile and protecting your whole-body health at the same time.

By taking this individualized, comfortable, and high-tech approach, The Dental Clinic will help you discover a newfound confidence in your health and smile.

With our help, you can start enjoying your smile today

General

Cosmetic

As a nationally-recognized cosmetic dentist and with over 15 years of changing smiles, we are proud to deliver the best care that cosmetic dentistry has to offer Yorkshire

If you feel unhappy about the appearance of your teeth, our smile transformation team will spend the time to deliver a solution unique to you. Our cosmetic dental care is meant to enhance your smile while at the same time improving your whole-body health.

Cosmetic services include…

  • Invisalign/Clear Correct
  • Teeth Whitening
  • Dental Veneers
  • Smile Makeovers
  • Full Mouth Reconstruction

Surgical
